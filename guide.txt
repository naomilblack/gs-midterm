HTML
------

- Navbar: Header with Logo and the Name of the fake theater.

- Movie Title and Instructions: H1/2/3 with the title of the movie (Scorcher VI: Global Meltdown) and the instructions to choose a seat. 

- Theater Seats Selection Box: Main content box that hold the "Screen" element and the individual seats. The rows will contain the same amount of seats, no matter the screen 
  size.

- Trailers: Div below the seats selection box that allows the user to see a trailer for the selected movie or other movies.

Container: class="container";
Seats: class = "seat" + "available" or "reserved";
